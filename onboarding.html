Getting Started

Create Your Account

Get up and running in three quick steps.

1

Sign Up

Tap Sign Up on the login screen. Enter your name, email, and password. Select your role — Coach or Athlete — and your group (Combined, Men’s, or Women’s).

2

Confirm Your Email

Check your inbox for a confirmation link and click it to activate your account.

3

Log In

Return to the app and sign in. You’ll land on your personalized dashboard.

Benchmarks

46 standard CrossFit benchmarks (Girl WODs, Strength, Cardio) with auto-fill templates.

▶ Posting a WOD

  • Go to Home and tap Post Today’s WOD, or use + New WOD on the Program tab
  • Optionally select a Benchmark Template to auto-fill name, type, and movements
  • Set the date, workout type (For Time, AMRAP, etc.), and group (Combined, Men’s, Women’s)
  • Add movements with reps and notes (e.g., “Rx: 95/65”)
  • Tap Post WOD — athletes will see it on their dashboard

Custom Workouts

Traveling or doing your own thing? Log a custom workout any time — it shows up in your history with a distinct badge.

▶ Logging a Workout

  • Open Home — today’s WOD appears at the top with the full movement list
  • Tap Log Workout to open the logging form
  • Enter your weights for each movement, select RX or Scaled
  • Add your completion time, an optional photo, and any notes
  • Tap Save — your result appears in History and the Activity Feed

▶ Missed a Day?

  • Scroll down on Home to the Missed Workouts section
  • Past 7 days of unlogged WODs appear here with a + Log button
  • Tap to log your result retroactively

Tips

Quick Tips

★

One Result Per Day

You can log one workout per day. Logging again on the same day updates your existing entry.

★

Edit or Delete Anytime

Made a mistake? Find the workout in your History tab and tap Edit or Delete.

★

PRs Are Automatic

When a coach posts a benchmark WOD and you log a result, your best time is automatically tracked as a PR.

★

Forgot Your Password?

Tap Forgot password? on the login screen. A reset link will be sent to your email.
